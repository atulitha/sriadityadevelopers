Sree Pavitra

Luxury Villas & Flats

Sree Pavitra is a prestigious gated community by Sri Aditya Developers, offering premium villas and flats in Kakinada Smart City, near Sarpavaram Junction, close to SRMT Mall and Bapanna Dhora Gosala. This luxurious project features 100% Vastu-compliant plots and a clear title, ensuring trust and peace of mind. Residents benefit from top-notch infrastructure, including electricity, water supply, drainage system, blacktop roads, and a welcoming entrance arch. The community is enhanced with lush avenue plantations, a secure compound wall, 24x7 CCTV surveillance, a landscaped park, and a dedicated children's play area — offering a perfect blend of comfort, security, and elegant living.
